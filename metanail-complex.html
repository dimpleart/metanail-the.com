Metanail Complex Ingredients 

The efficacy of Metanail Complex is rooted in its rich blend of natural ingredients, each meticulously chosen for its unique benefits to nail and foot health. The ingredients used in the formation of Metanail Complexare mentioned below:

1

Witch Hazel

**Witch Hazel** is a versatile botanical extract known for its soothing properties. Often used in skincare products, it helps reduce inflammation, tighten the skin, and can aid in healing minor skin irritations. In the context of nail health, it can provide relief from any inflammation around the nail bed.
2

Scots Pine

**Scots Pine** is a coniferous tree whose extracts are rich in antioxidants. These antioxidants play a crucial role in protecting the skin and nails from environmental stressors, ensuring they remain healthy and robust.
3

Horsetail Extract

**Horsetail Extract** is derived from the horsetail plant and is renowned for its high silica content. Silica is essential for strengthening nails, preventing brittleness, and promoting overall nail health.
4

Gotu Kola

**Gotu Kola** is a medicinal herb that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. It supports skin's natural protective function, ensuring the skin around the nails remains healthy and resilient.
5

Rosemary

**Rosemary** is more than just a culinary herb; it's packed with antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds. For nails, it can help improve circulation, ensuring that nails receive the nutrients they need to grow strong.
6

Pelargonium Graveolens

**Pelargonium Graveolens**, commonly known as geranium, offers a range of skincare benefits. It helps in balancing the skin's natural oils, ensuring the cuticles remain moisturized and healthy.
7

Glycerin

**Glycerin** is a humectant that attracts moisture to the skin. By keeping the skin around the nails hydrated, glycerin ensures that nails remain flexible and less prone to breakage.
8

Lemon Peel Extract

**Lemon Peel Extract** is rich in vitamin C and other antioxidants. These compounds help in repairing damaged nails and also play a role in collagen production, which is vital for nail growth.
9

Aloe Vera

**Aloe Vera** is a powerhouse when it comes to skin and nail care. Its moisturizing and healing properties ensure that nails and the surrounding skin remain hydrated and free from irritations.
10

Organic Green Tea

**Organic Green Tea** is packed with polyphenols, which are known for their antioxidant properties. These antioxidants protect nails from damage caused by free radicals, ensuring they remain healthy and vibrant.
